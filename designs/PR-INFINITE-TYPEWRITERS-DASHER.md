### üìö The Dasher Philosophy Explained

Don tells Palm the story of David MacKay:

**Dasher Mechanics:**
- Don't type ‚Äî STEER through probability space
- Common letters are BIG targets (easy to reach)
- Rare letters are SMALL (harder but possible)
- Continuous navigation like driving
- "Inaccurate gestures can be compensated by later gestures"

**Information Theory:**
- Shannon limit: ~1 bit per character for English
- Keyboards waste ~5-6 bits per keypress
- Dasher approaches theoretical efficiency
- Arithmetic coding in reverse

**Accessibility:**
- "Write with any muscle"
- Eye gaze, breath, head movement
- Constraints drove innovation
