## Why This Matters for MOOLLM

### 1. Sub-Rooms as Organization

Directories within directories create spatial meaning:
- `palm-nook/` is the home
- `palm-nook/study/` is where creative work happens
- `palm-nook/play/` is where chaos lives

### 2. Gift Agency

Characters can:
- Accept gifts (keep them)
- Re-gift to others
- Donate to public spaces
- Return to ACME for resources

This creates an ECONOMY of meaning.

### 3. Shared Spaces

The play room isn't just Palm's ‚Äî it's "contested by kittens."
The warm spot is "shared territory."
Ownership in MOOLLM can be collaborative.

### 4. Character Through Choices

Palm's personality emerges through:
- What they keep (meaningful items)
- What they share (fun items)
- What they return (nothing)
